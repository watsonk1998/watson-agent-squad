# Engram Integration

## What goes into Engram

Engram should store knowledge that is reusable beyond one note or one chat session:
- patterns
- anti-patterns
- rules
- handoffs
- architecture decisions
- important discoveries

## What stays in Obsidian

Obsidian should remain the primary place for:
- readable journals
- weekly and monthly reviews
- project notes
- long-form thinking
- navigational structure
